MagnaDesk Privacy Policy

Catio Productions  |  Last updated: April 2026

Overview

MagnaDesk is a desktop application for Windows. We are committed to your privacy. Catio Productions does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. All data MagnaDesk uses stays on your computer.

Data We Do Not Collect

MagnaDesk does not collect or transmit to Catio Productions:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Your location
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports
  • Any content from your accounts (mail, calendar, tasks, notes)

Account Tokens (Microsoft & Google)

If you connect a Microsoft or Google account to power widgets, MagnaDesk stores an OAuth access token and refresh token on your local machine. These tokens are encrypted using Windows DPAPI (Data Protection API) at the current-user scope, meaning they can only be decrypted by your Windows user account on your computer.

Tokens are used solely to make read-only requests to the following services on your behalf:

  • Microsoft: Mail (Outlook/Exchange), Calendar, Tasks / To-Do, OneNote
  • Google: Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Tasks

MagnaDesk never writes to, modifies, or deletes any data in your accounts. Tokens are never transmitted to Catio Productions or any third party other than the respective Microsoft or Google authentication servers.

You can revoke access at any time by removing the account in MagnaDesk Settings, or directly through your Microsoft account permissions or Google account permissions page.

Weather Widget

The Weather widget displays conditions for a location you choose manually. MagnaDesk does not access or detect your device's location. The city name you enter is sent to the Open-Meteo geocoding API to resolve coordinates, which are then used to fetch weather data. Open-Meteo is a free, open-source service that does not require an account or API key.

Open-Meteo's privacy policy is available at open-meteo.com/en/terms.

Web Image Sources (Unsplash, Pexels, Wallhaven)

If you add API keys for Unsplash, Pexels, or Wallhaven to download wallpaper images, those keys are stored locally on your machine encrypted with Windows DPAPI. Search queries and image requests are sent directly from your computer to those services. Catio Productions never sees your API keys or search terms.

Each service has its own privacy policy: Unsplash, Pexels, Wallhaven.

Local Storage

MagnaDesk stores your settings, canvas items, wallpaper configuration, and encrypted tokens in your Windows user profile (%AppData%\MagnaDesk). This data never leaves your computer unless you explicitly copy it.

Children's Privacy

MagnaDesk is a general-purpose desktop productivity application. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we will update this document and note the new date above. Continued use of MagnaDesk after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
