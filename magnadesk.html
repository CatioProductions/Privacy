MagnaDesk Privacy Policy

Catio Productions  |  Last updated: May 4, 2026

1. Overview

MagnaDesk is a desktop application for Windows. We are committed to your privacy. Catio Productions does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. All data MagnaDesk uses stays on your computer.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

MagnaDesk does not collect or transmit to Catio Productions:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Your location
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports
  • Any content from your connected accounts (mail, calendar, tasks, notes)

MagnaDesk does not contain advertising, third-party trackers, or analytics SDKs. No outbound network traffic is generated by MagnaDesk except as described in Sections 3, 8, and 9 of this policy.

3. Account Integrations

MagnaDesk widgets can connect to Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace accounts to display your own data on your desktop canvas. All access is initiated by you via the standard OAuth 2.0 sign-in flow operated by Microsoft or Google. MagnaDesk never sees your password.

4. Limited Use of Google User Data

MagnaDesk's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically, MagnaDesk does not:

  • Use Google user data to serve advertisements.
  • Transfer Google user data to others except as necessary to provide or improve user-facing features that are prominent in the application's user interface.
  • Allow humans to read Google user data, except (a) with the user's affirmative consent for specific messages, (b) when necessary for security purposes such as investigating abuse, (c) to comply with applicable law, or (d) where the data has been aggregated and anonymized for internal operations.
  • Sell Google user data.

5. Account Tokens — Storage and Encryption

When you connect a Microsoft or Google account, MagnaDesk stores the resulting OAuth access token and refresh token on your local machine, encrypted using Windows Data Protection API (DPAPI) at the current-user scope. This means the tokens can only be decrypted by your Windows user account on your computer.

Tokens are stored in %AppData%\MagnaDesk\ and are used solely to make read-only requests to Microsoft Graph or Google APIs on your behalf. Tokens are never transmitted to Catio Productions or any third party other than the respective Microsoft or Google authentication and resource servers.

6. Local Caching of Account Data

To keep widgets responsive and to allow brief offline display, MagnaDesk may cache the most recently fetched items shown by each widget in %AppData%\MagnaDesk\. Cached account data is encrypted with Windows DPAPI at the current-user scope. The cache is short-lived: each widget refreshes its cached entries on its normal refresh interval and on application startup.

Cached account data is automatically deleted when you disconnect the corresponding account in MagnaDesk Settings or when you uninstall MagnaDesk.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

OAuth tokens and any cached widget data are retained on your local machine until one of the following happens, at which point they are deleted from disk:

  • You remove the account in MagnaDesk Settings (the Disconnect action clears tokens and the widget cache for that account).
  • You uninstall MagnaDesk (all data under %AppData%\MagnaDesk\ is removed).
  • You revoke MagnaDesk's access on the provider side (revoked tokens are discarded the next time MagnaDesk attempts to use them).

You can revoke access at any time without going through MagnaDesk:

  • Microsoft: Microsoft account permissions
  • Google: Google account permissions

Because Catio Productions does not receive or store any of your account data on our servers, there is no server-side deletion request needed — clearing the data on your local machine is the complete deletion process.

8. Weather Widget

The Weather widget displays conditions for a location you choose manually. MagnaDesk does not access or detect your device's location. The city name you enter is sent to the Open-Meteo geocoding API to resolve coordinates, which are then used to fetch weather data from Open-Meteo.

Open-Meteo is a free, open-source weather service that does not require an account or API key. Their privacy policy is available at open-meteo.com/en/terms.

The Weather Radar widget fetches public radar imagery from the U.S. National Weather Service for a station you select. No personal data is sent in those requests.

9. Web Image Sources (Unsplash, Pexels, Wallhaven)

If you choose to add API keys for one or more wallpaper image services, those keys are stored locally on your machine, encrypted with Windows DPAPI. Search queries and image download requests are sent directly from your computer to the chosen service. Catio Productions never sees your API keys, your search terms, or the images returned.

Each service operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Unsplash Privacy Policy
  • Pexels Privacy Policy
  • Wallhaven Privacy Policy

10. Local Storage Summary

MagnaDesk stores the following on your computer in %AppData%\MagnaDesk\:

  • Application settings and preferences
  • Canvas items (sticky notes, drawings, photos, widgets, layout)
  • Wallpaper configuration
  • Encrypted OAuth tokens (Section 5)
  • Encrypted widget caches, if any (Section 6)
  • Encrypted user-supplied API keys for image sources (Section 9)
  • License information

None of this data leaves your computer unless you explicitly copy or share it.

11. Children's Privacy

MagnaDesk is a general-purpose desktop productivity application and is not directed to children under the age of 13. Catio Productions does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to MagnaDesk through a connected Microsoft or Google account, please contact us at the address below and we will help you ensure the relevant local data is removed.

12. Changes to This Policy

For material changes affecting how account data is handled, we will additionally surface a notice within MagnaDesk on next launch. Continued use of MagnaDesk after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
