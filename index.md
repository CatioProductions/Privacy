Privacy Policy
Effective date: March 29, 2026  ·  Applies to all Catio Productions apps
No personal data collected
Overview
Catio Productions ("we", "our", or "us") develops and publishes Windows desktop applications distributed through the Microsoft Store.
We respect your privacy. Our apps are designed to function entirely on your local device, and we do not collect, transmit, store, 
or share any personal information.


Data we do not collect
Our apps do not collect, access, or transmit any of the following:

— Personal identifiers (name, email, address, phone number)
— Device identifiers or hardware information
— Location data
— Usage analytics or telemetry
— Files, documents, or content on your device
— Browsing history or activity outside the app
— Payment or financial information

Local storage
Some apps may save settings or preferences locally on your device (such as window positions or user configurations). This data never 
leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-party services
Our apps do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Purchases and licensing are handled entirely by the 
Microsoft Store, and are subject to Microsoft's privacy policy. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Children's privacy
Our apps do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, 
our apps are safe for all ages.

Changes to this policy
If our data practices change in the future, this policy will be updated and a new effective date will be posted. We encourage you to 
review this page periodically.
